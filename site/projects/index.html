PROPULSION · TEST HARDWARE · ACTIVE

HTP / Jet-A Bipropellant Test Stand

A full test stand for a high-test-peroxide / Jet-A bipropellant rocket engine, built for accurate, repeatable thrust and combustion data.

  • Structural analysis of stand plates and a floating engine-mount plate isolating axial thrust for clean load-cell readings
  • Instrumentation design: pressure transducers, thermocouples, and pancake load cells with injector pressure taps and chamber sensor placement
  • Standoff-tube thermal analysis and dissimilar-metal weld design (ER309L for 316 SS to carbon steel)
  • Components sourced from McMaster-Carr, Swagelok, and Omega

Pancake Load Cell Signal Chain

A noise-immune analog front end for thrust measurement, converting a load-cell voltage into a current loop for reliable transmission to a DAQ.

  • Integrated into the broader test-stand data acquisition system
